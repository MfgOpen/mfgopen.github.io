ManufacturingOpen

Start here

Interested in engineering and manufacturing? But how do you jump in? We want to usher in a new era of open and accessible engineering and manufacturing engagement and education.

Join our Discord

Training without paywalls

Industrial and technical training has been hidden for far too long behind expensive gatekeeping and paywalls. We are committed to tearing down these walls.

We love open source

Open source development has revolutionized the software world. We believe that it is time to bring that same culture and progress to manufacturing.

Relationships not content

Learning does not come from videos or textbooks. Engineering and manufacturing is an inherently collaborative activity. Everyone has something of value to share. We should build systems to encourage long-term educational relationships.
